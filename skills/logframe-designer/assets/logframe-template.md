## Before you finalise — gaps checklist

- [ ] Every outcome has at least one indicator
- [ ] Every indicator has a defined MoV the team can really maintain
- [ ] Every baseline is real or marked `[TO CONFIRM — baseline survey]`
- [ ] Every target is grounded in its baseline and realistic for the budget
- [ ] No output is masquerading as an outcome
- [ ] No orphan activities (each serves a stated result)
- [ ] Assumptions column filled with real external conditions
- [ ] Disaggregation decided and safe to collect (consent for sensitive categories)
- [ ] Internal sign-off before this goes into a proposal or report
